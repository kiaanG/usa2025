USA Trip Itinerary

May 1, 2025 - May 30, 2025

Day 1: May 1, 2025 - Arrival in Atlanta

Arrive in Atlanta from Mumbai. Check into your hotel and settle in. Spend the evening exploring Centennial Olympic Park and dining at a local restaurant.

Day 2-4: May 2-4, 2025 - Exploring Atlanta

Visit the Georgia Aquarium, World of Coca-Cola, and Martin Luther King Jr. National Historical Park. On the last day, enjoy a stroll through the Atlanta Botanical Garden before your evening flight to Orlando.

Flight: Atlanta (ATL) to Orlando (ORL)

Day 5-8: May 5-8, 2025 - Orlando Theme Parks

Spend three days exploring Walt Disney World parks: Magic Kingdom, Epcot, and Disney's Hollywood Studios. Dedicate one day to Universal Studios for thrilling rides and attractions.

Day 9: May 9, 2025 - Relax and Flight to Seattle

Spend the morning relaxing in Orlando before your evening flight to Seattle. Check into your hotel upon arrival.

Flight: Orlando (ORL) to Seattle (SEA)

Day 10: May 10, 2025 - Exploring Seattle

Visit the Boeing Factory, Amazon HQ, and Starbucks HQ. Enjoy an evening at Pike Place Market.

Day 11-18: May 11-18, 2025 - Alaska Cruise

Board your cruise on the Norwegian Encore. Explore destinations like Juneau, Skagway, Glacier Bay, and Ketchikan. Disembark in Seattle on the morning of May 18.

Flight: Seattle (SEA) to San Francisco (SFO)

Day 19-20: May 19-20, 2025 - San Francisco

Visit Golden Gate Bridge, Alcatraz Island, and Fisherman's Wharf. Take a trip to Apple HQ for a tech-inspired experience.

Day 21: May 21, 2025 - Travel to Los Angeles

Fly to Los Angeles. Spend the evening exploring Hollywood Boulevard and the Walk of Fame.

Flight: San Francisco (SFO) to Los Angeles (LAX)

Day 22-23: May 22-23, 2025 - Los Angeles Highlights

Visit Griffith Observatory, Santa Monica Pier, and Getty Center. Spend a day at Six Flags Magic Mountain for thrilling roller coasters.

Flight: Los Angeles (LAX) to New York (NYC)

Day 24: May 24, 2025 - Flight to New York City

Arrive in NYC and settle into your hotel. Take an evening walk through Times Square.

Day 25-26: May 25-26, 2025 - NYC Exploration

Visit the Statue of Liberty, Empire State Building, and Central Park.

Day 27: May 27, 2025 - Trip to Buffalo

Fly to Buffalo and visit Niagara Falls. Explore local attractions before returning to your hotel.

Flight: New York (NYC) to Buffalo (BUF)

Day 28: May 28, 2025 - Washington, D.C.

Travel to Washington, D.C., and explore White House, Capitol Building, the Smithsonian Museums and National Mall.

Flight: Buffalo (BUF) to Washington DC (IAD)

Day 29-30: May 29-30, 2025 - Return to Mumbai

Spend the final day shopping and relaxing in Washington, D.C. Depart for Mumbai on the evening of May 30, concluding your memorable trip.

Flight: Washington DC (IAD) to Mumbai (BOM)
